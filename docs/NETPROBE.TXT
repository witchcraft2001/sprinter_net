NETPROBE.EXE - Low-level ESP diagnostic

Purpose
  Checks that the Sprinter-WiFi card and its firmware respond.

Usage
  NETPROBE.EXE

Role
  NETPROBE checks the card itself; it does not connect to Wi-Fi. A failed probe
  does not necessarily mean that the Wi-Fi settings are wrong.

Recommended use
  NETRESET.EXE
  NETPROBE.EXE
  NETUP.EXE

Exit status
  0  Probe completed.
  2  Sprinter-WiFi hardware was not found.
  3  ESP communication error.
